Speaker Portal

Pacific++ is a conference based around the C++ programming language. The conference is targeted at C++ professionals and enthusiasts in the pacific region. It is a high quality event where the primary goal is for participants to network and expand their knowledge as much as possible. Submissions close on the 17th of June 2018.

For further details about the conference, please check out the main conference website.

Expectations

As a speaker, you will be required to produce a 75 minute quality talk ideally focused on C++ best practise, modern techniques, tooling and libraries. The talk can be on any specific topic of your choosing, but is subject to approval from the organizers.

If you want to have an opportunity for the audience to ask you questions after your talk, please factor this in to the 75 minutes.

Remuneration, Benefits and Costs

Benefits:

  • Full conference access. This gives you a two day pass to the event with coffee, snacks and lunch
  • Exposure on the Pacific++ website, including biography and photo
  • Included in event marketing materials, emails and program guide
  • Networking/business development opportunities
